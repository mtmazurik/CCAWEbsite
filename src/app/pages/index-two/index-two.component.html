About Us: Cloud Computing Associates

Founded in 2017 in Portland, Oregon, we're perfectly positioned in the Pacific Northwest – geographically and philosophically situated between Microsoft and Google.
After more than 25 years working with Microsoft platforms and clouds, we made the strategic decision to go all-in on Google's AI ecosystem with Vertex and Gemini. Google Cloud had the advantage of arriving later to the market, bringing superior organization and a beautifully designed Material Design console that others simply lack.
Go is our go-to language (pun intended), and we love services like Cloud Run for lightning-fast container deployments. For mobile development, we're Flutter and Dart enthusiasts – building single-codebase solutions that deploy seamlessly to both Android and Apple app stores.
